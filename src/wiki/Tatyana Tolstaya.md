Tatyana Lvovna Tolstaya (Татьяна Львовна Сухотина-Толстая; 16 October 1864 NS – 21 September 1950), later Suhótina by marriage, was the eldest daughter of [[Leo Tolstoy]] and [[Sophia Tolstaya]], and a memoirist.

Born at [[Yasnaya Polyana]], she was close to her father throughout his life and left memoirs that constitute an important primary source for the Tolstoy household. After the Revolution she worked to preserve the legacy of Yasnaya Polyana, serving as director of the Tolstoy museum there before emigrating.

She outlived her father by forty years, dying in 1950.
