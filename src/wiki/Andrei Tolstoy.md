Andrei Lvovich Tolstoy (Андрей Львович Толстой; 18 December 1877 NS – 8 March 1916 NS) was a son of [[Leo Tolstoy]] and [[Sophia Tolstaya]], and a government official who served in the Russo-Japanese War.

Born at [[Yasnaya Polyana]], he pursued a career in state service rather than the literary or philosophical path associated with his father. He participated in the Russo-Japanese War of 1904–05.

He died in 1916, six years after his father, at the age of 38.
