Sergei Lvovich Tolstoy (Сергей Львович Толстой; 10 July 1863 NS – 23 December 1947) was the eldest son of [[Leo Tolstoy]] and [[Sophia Tolstaya]], and a composer, musicologist, and writer.

Born at [[Yasnaya Polyana]], he grew up surrounded by his father's literary and philosophical circle. He studied music seriously and became a recognised figure in Russian musical life, publishing studies of folk music and his father's relationship to it. He also wrote memoirs about life at Yasnaya Polyana that constitute a primary source for the domestic history of the Tolstoy household.

He outlived his father by thirty-seven years, dying in 1947.
