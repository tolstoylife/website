Maria Lvovna Tolstaya (Мария Львовна Оболенская, рожд. Толстая; 1871–1906) was a daughter of [[Leo Tolstoy]] and [[Sophia Tolstaya]]. She is identified in the TEI data as his second daughter. She married Prince Nikolai Obolenskhiy and took the name Obolenskaya.

She died in 1906, four years before her father, at the age of approximately 35. Exact birth and death dates are not recorded in the TEI source and require verification from a primary biographical source.
