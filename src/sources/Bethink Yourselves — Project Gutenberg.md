Project Gutenberg edition of Tolstoy's 1904 anti-war essay *Bethink Yourselves!* (Одумайтесь!), originally published in *The Times* (London) and as a pamphlet by the International Union (Ginn & Company, Boston, 1904). Translated by [[Vladimir Chertkov]] and an unnamed co-translator ("I. F. M.").

The text is a complete English translation of the essay in twelve numbered sections, with two footnotes. It is the primary source used to create the [[Bethink Yourselves!]] work page. The internal dates at the ends of sections XI and XII (OS "May 2, 1904" and "13 May, 1904") provide the composition dates; both predate the 1918 calendar reform and are converted NS by +13 days.
